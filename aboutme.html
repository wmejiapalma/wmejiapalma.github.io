About me

my story

The start

I remember my first programming language very vividly. I was nine years old when my sister showed my a simple hello world app in java. It was just system.out.println("Hello World") but it blew my MIND. After that encounter I knew I wanted to be a software developer in some way. Learning python and java in my spare time I made very basic fun tools to play with (pyturtle is fun).

Education

Apart from teaching myself in my spare time with tutorial videos and Codecademy, I also attended a computer science college called Neumont College of Computer Science. This year I am planning on graduating in September with a bachelor's in Software Engineering. I love software engineering and the courses at this school have made me an expert in many of them. From containerization, cloud architecture, and databases to webpages with React or even android applications with android studio.

Whats Next?

My immediate goal is to continue growing my technical abilities while also refining and building on my existing projects to enhance my craft. I'm always excited to collaborate with like-minded individuals who share my passion for software engineering and creating innovative solutions. If you are an employer looking for a passionate and dedicated software engineer, I would love to hear from you. Please feel free to reach out via the contact information provided on my website to discuss any potential opportunities. Thank you for considering me for any upcoming roles. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website and taking the time to learn more about me.
